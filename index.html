Bane

Fighter and Lover, Revolutionary and Humanitarian

Bane in his trademark stylish fur coat, preparing to address Gotham

Bane was one of the great humanitarians of our time. Here is a brief list of his remarkable achievements and skills:

  • Born and raised in the Pit, a hellish prison in the desert of the Middle East, located 50 feet below the surface.

    • "Oh, you think darkness is your ally. But you merely adopted the dark; I was born in it, moulded by it."

  • Mercilessly sacrified himself to save the life of a young girl and her mother - at terrible personal cost. Suffered horrific facial deformities and permanent damage as a result of the prisoner's attack
  • Expert mercenary and proficient in corporate espionage- contract to started a coup in West Africa, resulting in significant resource gain for client
  • Crashed a CIA plane and extracted high level VIP target - with no survivors!

    • "No one cared who I was until I put on the mask"

  • Outspoken critic of massive wealth inequality and industrial-prison complex. Plotted revolution to give power back to the people and redistribute wealth from the billionaires to the working class

    • "Peace has cost you your strength. Victory has defeated you!"

  • Expert in hand to hand combat, demolitions, and covert military operations

    • "Ah yes, I was wondering what would break first! Your spirit... or your body!"

  • Powerful and charismatic speech giver
  • Died as a martyr at the hands of the power-hungry, controlling billionaire ruling class. His sacrifice for the common people will not be forgotten
